case studies

'

Goal Time is a new way to bet on football that lets users stake money on the goal times in big matches. Goal Time use pinpoint to drive ‘appointment to play’ traffic to their mobile site which offers both mobile sign-up and game play. The team use pinpoint to target prospective users by relevant postcode in the immediate run-up to big featured matches and have seen both strong cost per click and propensity to deposit CPAs from their pinpoint traffic with results 5 x stronger than mobile display

'

MiniCabster worked with pinpoint to drive download of their taxi app in Manchester and saw results sixteen times more effective than conventional banners; one in two users who interacted downloaded the app, cost per install was below £3 and MiniCabster picked up valuable business travellers as a result. MiniCabster recommend pinpoint as a new mobile advertising solution.

'

For Worldwide Timeshare Hypermarket targeting the pinpoint audience whilst actually on holiday is a great way to reach their prospects in an optimal context. The team already used various forms of geo-targeting but pinpoint provides great accuracy, provides near instant results and details of audience size. What’s more, with pinpoint WTH can also target people who have been to a particular location in the last 30 days so they can also reach people on their return from a holiday location. Matching the target location (the Canaries) to relevant timeshare offers saw them achieve a high CTR and excellent metrics for a group of almost entirely first-time visitors to their site.

'

As part of the Mirror’s campaign to encourage the UK electorate not to waste their right their vote, the marketing team worked with pinpoint to target the public in ten of the top identified swing constituencies for the 2015 general election. On the Tuesday 48 hours before polling day we sent pinpoints to constituents including Bolton North East, Dudley South, and Dewsbury County promoting a bespoke political content on the Mirror’s mobile site.
