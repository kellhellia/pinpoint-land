case studies

'

We're using pinpoint to drive quality traffic to Goal Time's mobile site which offers both mobile sign-up and game play. Goal Time is an exciting new way to bet online on football that lets its community of users stake money on the goal times in big matches, the best part of the game! We use pinpoint to target prospective users by postcode in the immediate run up to big matches and have seen both strong cost per click and propensity to deposit from our pinpoint traffic.

'

We used pinpoint to drive download of our app in Manchester and saw results sixteen times more effective than conventional banners; one in two users who interacted downloaded the app, cost per install was below £3 and we picked up valuable business travellers as a result. We heartily recommend pinpoint as a new mobile advertising solution.

'

We are targeting pinpoint users whilst on holiday in certain locations as it is a great way to reach our relevant audience in optimal context. The team already used various forms of geo targeting but pinpoint provides great accuracy, provides near instant results and details of audience size. What’s more with pinpoint we can target people who have been to a particular location in the last 30 days so we can also reach people on their return from a holiday location. Matching the target location to relevant timeshare offers we achieved a high CTR and good metrics for a group of almost entirely first-time visitors to our site.

'

As part of the Mirror’s campaign to encourage the UK electorate not to waste their right their vote, the marketing team worked with pinpoint to target the public in ten of the top identified swing constituencies for the 2015 general election. On the Tuesday 48 hours before polling day we sent pinpoints to constituents including Bolton North East, Dudley South, and Dewsbury County promoting a bespoke political content on the Mirror’s mobile site.
